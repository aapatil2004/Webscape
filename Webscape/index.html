ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the space agency of the Government of India, and detailed information about specific ISRO employees or their home addresses would not be publicly available for privacy and security reasons. ISRO is involved in various space-related activities, including satellite launches, space exploration, and space research.

About Chandrayan 3

Chandrayaan-3 is India's third lunar mission that aims to achieve a soft landing on the Moon near the south pole region1. The spacecraft consists of an orbiter, a lander and a rover1. The launch of Chandrayaan-3 was originally planned for 2020, but was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic2. The latest information suggests that the launch will take place in the third quarter of 20222. The lander and rover are expected to land on the Moon in August 2022.
